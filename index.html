__ About me
UI Design App Design Web Design Design Systems UX Design Product Design Branding Illustration

Started with pixel art — drawing cars, because of course. That obsession turned into something bigger: figuring out how things work and how to make them better.

Websites, e-commerce, digital marketing, then 5+ years deep in blockchain and crypto. Different industries, same process every time — get close to the problem, understand the user, then design.

These days I'm co-building a mobile app startup from the ground up. Solo designer on the product, which means UX, UI, design system, and enough PM work to keep things moving.

Still illustrate when the mood hits.
